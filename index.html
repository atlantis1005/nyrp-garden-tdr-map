New York Restoration Project Garden Map for Transferrable Development Right Potential
Github

This map shows 14 gardens that were identified as high priority by the New York Restoration Project (NYRP) for potential transferrable development rights (TDR) transactions.

This map also shows the abutting properties (tax lots that share at least 10 contiguous feet the requirement for a TDR transaction).

Use the dropdown menu to zoom into a particular garden
