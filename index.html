New York Restoration Project Garden Map for Transferrable Development Rights

This map shows 14 gardens that were identified as high priority by the New York Restoration Project (NYRP) for potential transferrable development rights (TDR) transactions. This map is an spatial demonstration of a few of the variables that are relevant to TDR transactions. Full context and analysis of these garden sites can be found in the TDR database here.

Each garden is surrounded by abutting lots that may be able to receive development rights.

Use the dropdown menus to zoom into a particular garden and it's abutting properties.

Owner, %Built FAR, and "Chance of Redevelopment" information can be found by clicking on the abutting lots with the Soft Site Analysis Shown
